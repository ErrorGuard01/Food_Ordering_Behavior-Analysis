# 🍔 Customer Food Ordering Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Problem Statement

Food delivery platforms like Swiggy/Zomato operate in a highly competitive environment where understanding customer behavior is critical for increasing revenue, improving customer retention, and optimizing operations.

This project analyzes **50,000 food orders** to uncover patterns in customer behavior, spending habits, and ordering trends.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Identify peak ordering times
* Understand customer spending behavior
* Analyze impact of discounts and weather
* Study repeat vs new customer patterns
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
    - Pandas,
    - Matplotlib,
    - Seaborn,
    - Numpy
* SQL (PostgreSQL)
* Power BI Desktop
* ChatGPT
* Kaggle


---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains 50,000 records with features like:

* Order time (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night)
* Cuisine, meal type, restaurant type
* Order value, discounts, delivery fee
* Customer mood, hunger level
* Repeat order behavior
* Weather conditions

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Majority of orders occur during **Evening and Night**, indicating peak demand periods
* **Night orders have higher average value**, suggesting larger or group orders
* Discounts influence **customer retention more than spending**
* Repeat customers contribute significantly to revenue stability
* Orders increase during **rainy conditions**, showing higher dependency on delivery
* Emotional states like *celebrating* lead to higher spending

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase delivery partner availability during **evening peak hours**
* Promote **high-value combos during night time**
* Use discounts strategically to **retain customers instead of overusing them**
* Introduce **emotion-based recommendations** (e.g., celebration offers)
* Optimize operations during **rainy conditions** to handle demand spikes

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can help food delivery platforms improve customer experience, optimize operations, and increase revenue through targeted strategies.
